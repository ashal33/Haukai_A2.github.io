Privacy Statement


This pricacy statement will help you understand what information we collect from you as well as how we will use and protect it.
We collect personal information from you, including the following:
- Name
- Email address
- Contact information
- Location
 Personal information is gathered through our 'Reservation form'. We collect this information in order to process your booking made on our website and collect your location to ensure Google directions are correct.
Haukai is committed to keeping your personal data confidential by implementing the latest software to safeguard all information.
We will NOT disclose your information to ant third parties.
We keep your information for 24 hours after the reservation date at which point we securely destroy.
You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.
If you'd like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at info@haukai.co.nz, or or 09 401 4019, or 1 Island Street, Kerkeri, Bay of Islands.
